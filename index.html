History of Mwiti Bakers

Mwiti Bakers is a Kenyan bakery chain Headquaters in Uasin Gishu, Eldoret that specialises with the production of Bread, scones and Cakes.Founded By Peter Mwiti Muchai, in his parent house,together with his family support on June 1, 2022.It was established with the key objective of manufacturing and distributing bread,cakes and scones to the community. It also provide other wheat products such as biscuits and cookies...
